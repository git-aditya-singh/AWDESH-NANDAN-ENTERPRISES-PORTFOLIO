Our Verticals

Comprehensive EPC services across multiple sectors

Civil Construction Works

We provide comprehensive civil construction services for industrial facilities, including foundations, structural works, and infrastructure development. Our expertise ensures robust and reliable construction that meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

  • Industrial foundations and structural works
  • Equipment and machinery foundations
  • RCC structures, plant buildings, and utility areas

  • Site development and infrastructure construction
02

Mechanical Works

Our mechanical services cover fabrication, installation, and maintenance of industrial equipment and structures. We deliver end-to-end mechanical solutions from design to commissioning, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.

  • Fabrication and installation of steel structures
  • Equipment erection, alignment, and commissioning
  • Mechanical maintenance and shutdown support
  • Plant modification, revamping, and upgrade services

Electrical and Instrumentation Works

We provide integrated electrical and instrumentation services, including control systems, automation, and process instrumentation. Our expertise ensures seamless integration of electrical and instrumentation systems for optimal plant performance.

  • Industrial electrical commisioning and installation, loop checking

  • PLC, DCS & SCADA system support

Piping & Tubing Works

We specialize in industrial piping and tubing systems, handling everything from fabrication to installation and maintenance. Our services cover various materials and pressure ratings, ensuring safe and efficient fluid handling systems.

  • High-pressure and process piping
  • Stainless steel, carbon steel, and alloy tubing
  • Pipeline maintenance & integrity support
